Agency of Commodity

How not being tempted to redress the Economic Factor and Influences in nowadays practice of art. It is not anymore about art but about being an nowadays artist within its economic structure. You need to proof yourself and to do so you need to be available anytime without hesitation and too much thinking. Isn't more about performance and less about practice?
